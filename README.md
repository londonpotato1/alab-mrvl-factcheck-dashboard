# ALAB / MRVL AI Semiconductor Starter Dashboard

Static beginner-friendly dashboard for comparing two U.S.-listed semiconductor companies:

- Astera Labs, Inc. (`ALAB`)
- Marvell Technology, Inc. (`MRVL`)

The dashboard explains what each company sells, how it is used in AI data centers, why revenue may grow, what the latest financial numbers mean, and what risks beginners should watch. It uses primary sources first: company IR releases, SEC filings, and official NVIDIA / standards-body announcements. Market-price-derived valuation metrics should be refreshed before use.

## Open Locally

Open `index.html` directly in a browser, or serve the folder:

```bash
python3 -m http.server 8765 --bind 127.0.0.1
```

Then open:

```text
http://127.0.0.1:8765/
```

## Contents

- `ALAB` tab: plain-language business explanation, AI role, revenue growth, price burden, growth drivers, beginner risk checklist.
- `MRVL` tab: plain-language explanation of custom AI chips, optics, networking, revenue growth, data center exposure, beginner risk checklist.
- `Compare` tab: childlike analogies, simple business comparison, investor-fit framing, personality scorecard.

## Important Note

This is a research dashboard, not investment advice. All data should be re-verified before making financial decisions.
